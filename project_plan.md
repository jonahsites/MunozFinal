# 3 Coach Munoz — Soccer Training Website

## 1. Project Description
A professional soccer coaching business website for "3 Coach Munoz", a family-run training company founded in 2019. The website targets soccer players of all skill levels (especially youth) and their families, showcasing services, philosophy, and providing a booking/contact mechanism. Core value: help players "take their game to the next level" through personalized, technical soccer coaching.

## 2. Page Structure
- `/` - Homepage (Hero, Services overview, Stats, Testimonials, CTA)
- `/about` - About (Story, Mission, Vision, Philosophy, Team)
- `/services` - Services (All 5 training programs in detail)
